CommunityShare : 4 Super Easy Steps!

  1. JOIN CommunityShare!

    Create your profile. It only takes minutes!

  2. SEARCH

    Search for educators who may benefit from your expertise. There are many search parameters so you can narrow or widen your search. Your search will result in a list of matches (educator profiles) that you can explore.

  3. CONNECT

    Found an educator who may benefit from your expertise? Send a MESSAGE to connect with them.

  4. SHARE!

    After connecting via MESSAGES, you can organize a SHARE by clicking: CREATE A SHARE to confirm the share details, date, time and location to make the share a reality!

  5. Go SHARE!

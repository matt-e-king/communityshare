CommunityShare : 4 Super Easy Steps!

(1) JOIN CommunityShare!

Create your profile. It only takes minutes!

(2) SEARCH

Search for a community partner to visit your classroom. There are many search parameters so you can narrow or widen your search. Your search will result in a list of matches (partner profiles) that you can explore.

(3) CONNECT

Found a community partner who may fit your needs? Send a MESSAGE to connect with them.

(4) SHARE!

After connecting via MESSAGES, you can organize a SHARE by clicking: CREATE A SHARE to confirm the share details, date, time and location to make the share a reality!

Go SHARE!
